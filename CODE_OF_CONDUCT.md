# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or ability.

Be respectful, give actionable feedback, protect participant privacy, and assume good intent while addressing harmful behavior directly. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, publishing private information, or deliberately disrupting the project.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

This policy is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
